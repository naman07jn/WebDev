BUGATTI DIVO

The Bugatti Divo is a limited-production hypercar, limited to 40 units, named after French racer Albert Divo. It is a more track-focused version of the Chiron, with a W16 engine producing 1,500 horsepower, a focus on enhanced handling, and improved downforce, though its top speed is slightly limited to 380 kph (about 236 mph). Key features include a new aerodynamic design, updated suspension, and a focus on sharper, more agile handling compared to the Chiron.
